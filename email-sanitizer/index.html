Email Sanitizer - Gmail Only

Total: 0

Email Sanitizer - Gmail Only is a simple web-based tool that helps you clean your email list to keep only valid and neat @gmail.com addresses. This tool will automatically:

  • Filter only emails with the @gmail.com domain
  • Remove invalid characters like dots (.), plus (+), or other symbols in the local part of the email
  • Automatically remove duplicate emails
  • Allow you to easily paste, fix input, and copy results with one click

Suitable for:

  • Cleaning email data exports from Excel, Google Forms, or databases
  • Preparing email lists for newsletter campaigns or verification
  • Simplifying contact lists for consistency and error-free usage

With a lightweight and intuitive interface, this tool helps you save time and ensure a clean, valid, and ready-to-use email list.

Example:

Invalid input: x.y.z@gmail.com, x+y+z@gmail.com, Z!@#$%^&*()_+@gmail.com, zyz@yahoo.com
